Hey there!!! Welcome to "Go Student, Go!"

This game is about University. Our hero is the student who need to overcome some difficulties in order to graduate - finish the game. my friend, will help him do that. You need to pass 4 levels - freshman, sophomore, junior and senior years. Do not let the monsters catch if you want to pass the semester. If one of the monsters catches you - you failed the course. the game is Over. Collect as many A's as possible, but skip the F ones.

Use ⇦ ⇨ ⇧ to move.
